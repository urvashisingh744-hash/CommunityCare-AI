- Government portals  
- Helplines  
- Verified community databases  

## 5. Safety Agent
Checks for:
- Accuracy  
- Pseudoscience  
- Wrong emergency advice  

## 6. Response Generation
Outputs clean, empathetic, stepwise instructions.
